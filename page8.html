Section 8 Animations (Shadows, transitions)

Shadows

Shadows are more of what we have already learned where all you right is box-shadow for a outline or text shadow for text in the style tag and for which item you want this to apply for. You also have to write in pixel measurements where the bigger the pixels the thicker the shadow.

Transitions

Transitions can mean a lot of things but in this website we are going to be focusing on hover transitions and for these all you do is write the length of the transition in the tag of the style tag you want this to apply to. And then write what you want to happen in the applied tag but instead with a :hover at the end. Ex: div:hover

This the end of this short section about animation. I know there is much more talk about but this are the animations I prefer. But feel free to experiment.
